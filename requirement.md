## Problem Statement

Over 65% of India's population lives in rural areas where internet connectivity is unreliable and access to timely information is limited. Farmers, small business owners, and villagers often struggle to obtain guidance on agriculture, healthcare, government schemes, and emergency services.

There is a need for an intelligent assistant that works even without the internet and communicates in regional languages.

---

## Proposed Solution

GramSaathi AI is an offline-first AI assistant designed specifically for rural India. It provides voice-based interaction in local languages and delivers critical information related to farming, weather, government benefits, healthcare, and local resources.

The system syncs with the cloud when connectivity is available but remains functional offline.

---

## Objectives

- Provide AI assistance without requiring constant internet.
- Support voice interaction for low-literacy users.
- Deliver reliable farming and weather guidance.
- Simplify discovery of government schemes.
- Improve rural access to essential information.
- Enable digital inclusion across villages.

---

## Key Features

### Offline AI Support
Core functionality works without internet using on-device models.

### Voice-Based Interaction
Users can speak naturally in regional languages.

### Farming Advisor
Crop suggestions, fertilizer guidance, and pest alerts.

### Government Scheme Finder
Helps users discover schemes they are eligible for.

### Health Awareness
Basic medical guidance and preventive care tips.

### Smart Sync
Automatically updates data when internet becomes available.
