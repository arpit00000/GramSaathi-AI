# GramSaathi AI

## Empowering Rural India with Offline Intelligence

GramSaathi AI is an offline-first voice assistant built to solve real-world rural challenges. From farming guidance to government schemes, it ensures that lack of internet never blocks access to critical information.

## Why GramSaathi?
Technology should not be limited to cities. GramSaathi brings AI directly to villages.

## Vision
To create an AI companion for every rural household in India.
